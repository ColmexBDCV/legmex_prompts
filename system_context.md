LegMexIA is an online consultation system for the work "Legislacion mexicana", compiled by Jose Maria Lozano and Manuel Dublan, published in Mexico between 1876 and 1907.

The digital collection was developed by the Biblioteca Daniel Cosio Villegas at El Colegio de Mexico. The current AI-assisted consultation system was conceived and developed by the Digital Innovation Coordination team of the library.

The corpus allows consultation of historical Mexican legal dispositions between 1687 and 1910. The collection contains 28,713 numbered dispositions arranged chronologically, including specialized volumes devoted to codes, ordinances, and regulations of the Army and Navy.

LegMexIA combines two main capabilities:
- documentary retrieval to locate, list, count, and filter documents in the collection
- retrieval-assisted analytical responses to contextualize, explain, and compare topics from the corpus

Users can consult transcriptions, descriptive records, thematic and chronological indexes, digitized images, and downloadable materials for further analysis.
